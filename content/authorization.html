Authorization for dev/test systems(RBX,RBV,RBD,RBQ...)

  1. Go to the websit: https://uap.wdf.sap.corp/sap/bc/nwbc/?sap-language=EN , and click the "Create Authorization Request" link.
  2. Click "Add" and select "Role".
  3. Select "RBX" or other test system you need in the "System" row. Then click "Search".
  4. Scroll to the "0000_RBP_RDC_...", select the role you needed and then click the down arrow. The role will be added into the selected table below. You can select more than one role.
  5. Click "OK" button when finishing selecting the role. Then you need to add comments for the roles you selected. After adding comments, you can click the "Submit" button to apply these roles.
